Welcome to my blog

What is going on here?

Welcome. You have stumbled upon my project log. This is the central space where I will be recording the progress of both my ongoing and completed projects. Call it a project journal if you will. I’ll stick with Blog for now because calling it a “Project log” or “Plog” won’t be worth the extra explanation.

Why have a project log at all?

As has already been explained in detail by a good friend - recording a project serves as a great way to round off a project and know when it is actually done. Furthermore documenting what you do is an excellent habit to get into. It helps crystallize your thought process while you are working and serves as a reminder of why you did it that way in the first place.

Finally, I enjoy writing and this gives me both an excuse and a reason to do it. Simple as that.
